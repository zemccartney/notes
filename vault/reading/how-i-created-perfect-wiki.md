>**simplicity wins**. The simpler and more understandable your product, the easier it is to sell and maintain. When you have a small team and limited resources, simplicity isn’t a luxury — it’s a necessity. It keeps you from drowning in features, endless requests, and tech debt.

I think about this all the time working on side projects, putting a premium on making your own life easier by trying to do less, to operationalize your work so it's easier to pick up when you inevitably put it down for longer than you wanted to
